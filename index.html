Fasterize bookmarklets

Drag links to your bookmark bar and uses them on your Fasterized website to activate/deactivate Fasterize.

After clicking the on/off bookmarklets, you should either:

  • do a full refresh of the page, CMD+SHIFT+F5 on most systems
  • empty your browser's cache
